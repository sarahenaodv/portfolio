    My work
    FLAT ILLUSTRATION
    About this project

    This illustration, crafted using a flat illustration technique, draws deep inspiration from the elegance and rhythm of Chinese calligraphy. The central figure is a striking tiger, rendered with bold, flowing lines that echo the dynamic brushstrokes of traditional ink art. Its form merges strength and fluidity, capturing both the power of the animal and the grace of calligraphic movement. Every element in the composition feels intentional, creating a sense of balance and quiet intensity.

    This piece stands as my favorite work because it encapsulates who I am as an artist. It doesn't just represent a visual idea, it carries my soul within its design and meaning.
